How Karigar AI Works

4 simple steps - fully on WhatsApp, no app download needed

1
🎤
Send a Voice Note
Worker sends a voice note on WhatsApp describing what skill they want to learn in their own language.

2
🤖
AI Understands & Responds
AI uses Speech-to-Text, then generates a short voice lesson back in the worker's regional language.
